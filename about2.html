Hi, I’m Molly McCann and I'm a data enthusiast who recently earned my Masters in Business Analytics - Sports Analytics from the University of Notre Dame. I have a deep passion for sports and a love for exploring data in all its forms.

Sport has shaped who I am, both on and off the field. As a former NCAA Division III soccer player, I’m especially drawn to the game of soccer and how data can drive better decisions in scouting, strategy, and player development. My goal is not only to uncover meaningful insights through data, but also to inspire others to see the beauty and potential of sport through analytics.

When I’m not diving into soccer data or watching my favorite teams, I enjoy creating visualizations and analyzing data on topics I find fascinating, from film to music. I love uncovering stories in the numbers and sharing insights that resonate with others.
